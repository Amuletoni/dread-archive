About the Archive

The Dread Archive exists to preserve and analyze the rich history of horror video games. From the pixelated terrors of the early era to the photorealistic nightmares of today, we believe horror gaming deserves serious critical examination.

Our database catalogs games across every subgenre of horror. Our reviews focus on gameplay and mechanics—how a game plays, not just what it means. Our analysis articles dive deep into the themes, symbolism, and cultural significance of horror's greatest achievements.
